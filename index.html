SOME FACTS

A breeding male American Goldfinch

The Washington State Bird is the American Goldfinch.

American Goldfinches live in open woodlands, such as weedy fields, and can also be spotted in suburban backyards. They primarily eat seeds, such as thistle, sunflowers, and asters.

The Great Blue Heron

The Great Blue Heron is the Seattle City Bird.

The Seattle City Bird was designated on March 17, 2003. It was done to raise awareness of the bird's habitat requirements.
